Proof of Concept (PoC) Development

Start with PoC of your software product to test potential success of your idea and exclude possible development and implementation risks. This would also allow selecting the most suitable tech stack to ensure the solution works as intended.

Proof of Concept (PoC) Development

Project Size: Medium to large/enterprise-level

Duration: From 2-3 days to a few months

Want to test your idea before putting large investments in full-scale software product development? Have a trial run of your project by conducting Proof of Concept. It allows checking the feasibility of your idea, how it will work in the real-world conditions, what risks, restraints, benefits and pitfalls you should expect on the way.

Opportunities
  • Ability to foresee the success of your idea and overall software development project
  • Selecting the most suitable technologies and tools to develop and implement the solution
  • Exact estimation and validation of product scope, duration, resources needed, and its budget
  • Minimizing financial risks and ability to predict and reduce possible expenses
  • Faster time-to-market of a product supported by a proven concept

Common Stages

  • Requirements analysis and the concept research
  • Defining the project scope, budget, and timeline
  • Selecting a technology stack that fits your business needs and requirements
  • PoC design and development
  • Product demo
  • Roadmap for further implementation
