Cooperation Models

How would you like to partner with us?

Fixed Price

Consider a fixed-price contract if your project is short-term and small scale while its scope, requirements, and deadlines can be clearly defined.

Time and Material

Benefit from greater flexibility throughout the SDLC being charged only for the time spent and resources allocated for your project.

Dedicated Team

Get the entire product development process under your control having a dedicated team of professionals to match the scale of your project.

Research and
Development (R&D)

Before committing all your resources to the project with such emerging technologies as AI, big data, etc., it’s always better to research and validate the new black.

Proof of Concept
(PoC) Development

Start with PoC of your software product to test potential success of your idea and exclude possible development and implementation risks. This would also allow selecting the most suitable tech stack to ensure the solution works as intended.

MVP Development

Check the viability of your product in real-life conditions by releasing a minimal viable product. This way you will see how users react to its functionality and what other features can be added and improved.
