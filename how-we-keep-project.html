Project planning

Have you ever asked yourself a question: What can I do to manage projects better, for my team, for our clients, for myself? That's a pretty formative question to yourself but an important one for the successful project manager.

Preparation of the project

During the preparation of the project we have to perform several useful steps into its planning to avoid run offs the budget, team overtimes and overall stressful situations. There are main ones: Initiation - the phase where we describe business flow of the project, describe user stories and confirm the main key points which should be done.

The next one is Planning, the step where you plan all necessary activities and tasks which should be performed during the project. For this Project Managers are using several techniques such as SMART, Agile, Scrum, Kanban or classical Waterfall. Usually the method of performing planning depends on the Project Requirements and can include more than one method of planning during one project.

The most interesting phase is Preparing of the project. Yeahh, it can be tough and even hard sometimes, but it is always challenging and captivating. During the preparation of the project one of the major points is to run the approved schedule, especially it’s the key point for the fixed-price projects.

Project Management is the effective tool for the fixed-price projects because it helps to keep all deadlines, earlier approved estimations and clients can be sure that the performed work will be done on time.

Sometimes during the working process the situations may occur when the team is not in the approved time schedule because of the hard level of the task or my some unexpected earlier situations. That’s life, everything can happen. How in this situation Project Management can help?

Remember, we have said before that PM is the process which is performed by one or several members of the team to connect the requirements from the client and convert them into the tasks that the team will perform. To avoid some blocks that may occur during the work during the preparation of the project, the team has daily calls to discuss all issues, questions and suggestions. Aso after each Sprint the team has retrospective, which is the process that has been discovered specially for the team to discuss the methodologies that have been used, ideas for the next sprint, what they will use in the next one, and what is better to leave in the sprint that had already gone.

Daily meetings, sprints, performed tasks and documentation that are prepared during the process help clients to be updated about the flow of the project and be sure that everything is on track. During this process we share with the Client all necessary documentation such as: Strategies, time reports, sprint review, technical solutions and other which is required

There may be situations that require data and necessary help from the Client. In this way we escalate the problem to the hire level and discuss the possible solutions together. Everything to perform great projects at the end.

Project Management is the process that succeeds during the performed workflow.
